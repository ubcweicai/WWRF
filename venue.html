Meeting Venue of WWRF 31

 

The UBC Robson Square is the venue of the 31th Meeting of the Wireless World Research Forum.

UBC Robson Square is a vibrant learning centre located in the heart of downtown Vancouver. Each year they host over 40,000 people who attend adult educational programs, meetings, conferences and public seminars. The professionally trained meeting staff and banquet chefs are happy to ensure the WWRF31 delegates a successful event.
