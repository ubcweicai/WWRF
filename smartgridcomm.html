Joint SmartGridComm - WWRF Workshop

 

A half-day workshop will be held jointly with IEEE SmartGridComm 2013 on Monday, 21 Oct. 2013 at the Renaissance Vancouver Harbourside Hotel. Attendance at the workshop is included in the WWRF31 registration fee, but only those WWRF31 attendees who pre-register for this workshop when they register for WWRF-31 by 6 October, 2013 will be admitted. If you have pre-registered for this workshop, please go to the IEEE SmartGridComm 2013 registration desk before the workshop to pick up a special badge that will admit you to the workshop. The regular workshop registration fee is payable if you register on-site.

Workshop Chairs: Prof. Teng Joon Lim (National University of Singapore), Prof. Panagiotis Demestichas (University of Piraeus) and Prof. Lutz Lampe (The University of British Columbia)
